+ Magnoliaceae

- Magnoliaceae
    usually called? Magnolia family
    how many? 7 genera
    219 species
    how do we tell? The leaves are simple and their placement alternates on the stem; the flowers are pretty and arranged singly, not in clusters. This family of plants is known for having their sexual parts arranged in a spiral on a cone-shaped base, like the scales wind up a pine cone. Excepting the two tulip trees, this family is pollinated by beetles.
  • Magnolia grandiflora - Southern magnolia / bull bay
