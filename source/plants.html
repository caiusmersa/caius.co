Families of Flowering Plants (angiosperms)

  • Anacardiaceae
      usually called? cashew / sumac family
      how many? 70 genera
      650 species
      how do we tell? -resin canals located in the inner fibrous bark
    • pepper tree - Schinus molle
      • pepper tree - Schinus molle on Wikipedia
      • origin? -native to arid areas of Northern South America and Peru's Andean deserts
        edible / healing? -the pink peppercorns are used in cooking
        -the Inca made a fermented drink from the sweet outer part of the fruit
        toxic? -fruit and leaves may be toxic to poultry, pigs, and calves
    • poison oak - Toxicodendron diversilobum
  • Magnoliaceae
      usually called? magnolia family
      how many? 7 genera
      219 species
      how do we tell? -leaves are simple and their placement alternates on the stem
      -flowers are pretty and arranged singly, not in clusters
      -sexual parts are arranged in a spiral on a cone-shaped base, like the scales winding up a pine cone
      -pollinated by beetles, except for the two tulip trees
    • Magnolia grandiflora - Southern magnolia / bull bay
    • Liriodendron tulipifera - American tuliptree
